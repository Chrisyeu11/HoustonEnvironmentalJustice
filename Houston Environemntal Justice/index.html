Environmental Justice

Ensuring fair treatment for all people in environmental matters

What we are about as Environmental Justice

Environmental justice is the fair treatment and meaningful involvement of all people, regardless of race, ethnicity, income, or education level, in the development, implementation, and enforcement of environmental laws, regulations, and policies.
